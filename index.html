Spam SMS Prediction

Spam SMS Prediction is a machine learning-based application designed to classify SMS messages as either spam or not spam. The system uses a variety of text-processing techniques to analyze the content of incoming messages and predicts their nature (spam or ham). The project leverages machine learning algorithms like Naive Bayes, Support Vector Machine (SVM), or Logistic Regression to train the model, ensuring high accuracy in predicting whether an SMS is a potential spam message. The goal is to help users filter unwanted or potentially harmful messages.
